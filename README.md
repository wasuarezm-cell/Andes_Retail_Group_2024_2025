## 📝 Project Overview
This repository contains the commercial performance analysis for **Andes Retail Group**, a retail company operating in Peru, Chile, and Colombia. The main objective of this project was to transform a scattered transactional dataset into an interactive and executive dashboard, designed with visual hierarchy principles and data storytelling to facilitate strategic decision-making.

## 🎯 Objectives Achieved
* Analyzed the evolution of revenue (USD 5.53M) and profitability during 2024 and 2025.
* Identified seasonal patterns, geographical gaps, and commercial impact across customer segments.
* Developed a structured business narrative using the **SCQA** (Situation, Complication, Question, Answer) framework.

## 🛠️ Tools and Technologies
* **Power BI Desktop:** Data modeling, DAX measure creation, and interactive visualization.
* **Power Query:** Data cleaning, validation, and profiling, including conditional column creation.
* **Jupyter Notebook / Google Colab:** Project documentation, analysis planning, and executive reporting.
* **Methodologies:** Data storytelling, F/Z pattern layout design, and executive communication.

## 🖥️ Dashboard Structure
The report was built to avoid cognitive overload, divided into two main views:

1. **Overview:** Provides a quick read of the business's health. Highlights key metrics, shows strong year-end seasonality, and displays sales volume leadership in Peru and Chile.
2. **Detail View:** Designed for dynamic exploration and diagnosis. Allows cross-filtering profitability against seasons, revealing that the winter revenue drop is a demand volume issue, not a cost structure problem.

## 💡 Key Findings and Insights
* **Critical Seasonality:** Summer sales triple winter sales. This pattern repeats consistently across all countries and categories.
* **Stable Profitability:** Despite seasonal revenue drops, the profit margin remains incredibly stable (~35%) across all segments (Premium, Standard, Economy).
* **Business Engine:** Tickets classified as "High Value" (*Venta Alta*) represent almost 72% of total revenue, despite making up less than half of the transactions.
* **Geographical Opportunity:** Colombia contributes only 24% of total revenue, representing the market with the largest room for penetration and growth.

## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations
Based on the data analyzed, the following recommendations were delivered to the executive board:
1. Design seasonal campaigns (bundles or cross-selling) specifically to sustain sales volume during the winter (June-August).
2. Execute an aggressive penetration strategy in Colombia, replicating the successful Premium and Standard customer retention models from Peru and Chile.
